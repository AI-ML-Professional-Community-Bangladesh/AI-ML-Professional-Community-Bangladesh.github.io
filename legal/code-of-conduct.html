Code of Conduct

AIML Professional Community Bangladesh is a volunteer-led, non-profit community. Our focus is on strengthening the local AI/ML community and ensuring a free, open-source, and collaborative space for professional growth. We do not charge learners or pay mentors.

Community Goal

  • Knowledge Sharing: Encouraging open-source contributions, sharing research, projects, and tutorials.
  • Mentorship & Support: Organizing Q&A sessions, workshops, and mock interviews to help early-career AI/ML practitioners.
  • Community Engagement: Facilitating discussions and arranging meetups.
  • Research & Development: Promoting study groups and research validation.

Code of Conduct

  1. Be Respectful & Inclusive
    • Treat all members with respect regardless of background or opinion.
    • No discrimination, harassment, or hate speech.
  2. Collaborate & Share Responsibly
    • Share accurate, open-source content with proper credit.
    • Avoid plagiarism.
  3. Support & Encourage
    • Give constructive feedback.
    • Be patient with beginners.
  4. No Spam, Promotions, or Self-Promotion
    • Avoid excessive self-promotion.
    • Projects are welcome if they provide community value.
  5. Keep Discussions Professional
    • Stay on-topic in discussions.
    • Resolve disagreements respectfully.
  6. Respect Privacy & Rules
    • Do not share personal info without consent.
    • Follow moderator instructions.
  7. Conflict of Interest
    • Do not violate NDAs or confidentiality agreements.
  8. Avoid Political Discussions
    • Focus only on AI/ML and professional topics.

Applies to: All contributors, members, admins, guests, speakers, organizers, and event attendees.
