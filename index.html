About Us

About Us

AIML Professional Community Bangladesh is a volunteer-led non-profit community. We do not charge learners or pay mentors. Our focus is to strengthen the local AI/ML community and ensure a free, open-source, and collaborative space for professional growth.

What We Offer

  • Free Membership – no fees, ever
  • Open Mentorship – led by experienced AI/ML professionals
  • Community Events – workshops, talks, and hackathons
  • Collaborative Reseach & Projects – contribute, learn, and grow together

Our Vision

To create a thriving, inclusive, and globally connected AI/ML talent pool from Bangladesh through open collaboration and continuous learning.

Our Goals

  • Bridge the gap between AI/ML learners, researchers, and industry professionals.
  • Promote ethical, inclusive, and impactful use of AI/ML technologies.
  • Foster open-source contributions and collaborative research from Bangladesh.
  • Empower underrepresented communities to participate in the AI/ML ecosystem.
  • Build global connections while strengthening local capabilities.
